About us


Every cat at our shelter is an individual being with personality, feelings, intrinsic worth and value. As such, they will be treated with respect and care in a safe environment. We started as an individual idea to protect street and home cats from homelessness, suffering from weather conditions, hunger and thirst, at first it was just a room in my house, and thanks to your donations and cooperation, we became a full team and we were able to provide more space and better possibilities for treating and caring for cats, and then searching for a suitable adopter for each cat to ensure a decent life to it.
